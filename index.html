FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about solar energy and our installation process.

How much does a solar system cost?

The average residential solar system costs between $15.000 and $40.000 before incentives. After the 30% federal tax credit and state rebates, the net cost is significantly lower. We providefree. detailed quotes tailored to your specific energy needs and roof configuration.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations are completed in 1-3 days. The entire process from initial consultation to system activation typically takes 6-8 weeks, which includes design, permitting, installation, and utility interconnection.

Will solar panels work on cloudy days?

Yes, solar panels still generate electricity on cloudy days. though at reduced capacity (typically 10-25% of peak output). Modern panels are designed to capture diffused light effectively. Battery storage helps bridge any gaps in generation.

What happens during a power outage?

Standard grid-tied systems shut down during outages for safety reasons. However, if you have a battery storage system. it will automatically provide backup power to your home. We recommend battery storage for customers in areas with frequent outages.

Do solar panels require maintenance?

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. We recommend an annual inspection and occasional cleaning to remove dust and debris. Our maintenance plans include monitoring, cleaning, and performance checks to ensure your system operates at peak efficiency.
